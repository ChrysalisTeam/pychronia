NON JQUERY PAGE TRANSITIONS LIGHTWEIGHT
In the "data-ftrans" attribute you can assign any transition you want:
Slide   Slide Up   Slide Down    Fade   Flip   Pop   
You can also add reverse to the transition. (data-ftrans="slide reverse"):
You can assign transition effects to the nav buttons by just setting the navB var:

<script type="text/javascript">
navB = "slide reverse";
</script>
